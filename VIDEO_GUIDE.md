## 🎥 Recording Tips

1. **Clear Audio**: Use good microphone
2. **Clean Desktop**: Close unnecessary windows
3. **Smooth Navigation**: Know where to click
4. **Explain as You Go**: Narrate each action
5. **Show Results**: Wait for operations to complete
6. **Highlight Features**: Point out key functionality
